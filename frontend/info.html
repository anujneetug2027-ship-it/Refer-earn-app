About AmbikaShelf Enterprises

AmbikaShelf Enterprises is a privately operated digital enterprise founded and managed by Anuj Chauhan. The organization focuses on building scalable consumer platforms, technology services, and experimental artificial intelligence solutions.

The company was established with the vision of combining commerce, technology, and innovation into a unified ecosystem that benefits customers, startups, and partner businesses.

Brands & Divisions

AmbikaShelf Solutions — Web development, application building, branding, and startup enablement services.

AmbikaShelf AI — Research-driven initiative focused on personalized AI systems and business automation.
